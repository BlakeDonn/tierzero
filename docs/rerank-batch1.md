# BiS Re-Ranking Batch 1: Caster DPS + Healers

Specs covered: fire-mage, arcane-mage, frost-mage, shadow-priest, holy-priest, discipline-priest, elemental-shaman

Sources consulted:
- Wowhead TBC Classic pre-raid BiS guides (per-spec)
- Warcraft Tavern TBC pre-raid BiS guides
- Icy Veins TBC Classic pre-raid gear guides
- Wowhead tooltip API for item verification

## fire-mage

### Reorders
- **neck**: Manasurge Pendant moved to #1 (was #2). Guides consistently rank it as BiS neck for mages.
- **back**: Shawl of Shifting Probabilities moved to #1 (was #2). More accessible than Cloak of the Necropolis (Classic Naxx).
- **ring1**: Seer's Signet moved to #1 (was #3). Higher raw spell power than Ashyen's Gift; guides favor it.
- **trinket1**: Icon of the Silver Crescent moved to #1 (was #2). On-use SP burst valued higher than Quagmirran's Eye passive.
- **wand**: The Black Stalk moved to #1 (was #2). Higher SP than Nether Core's Control Rod.

### Additions
- **offhand**: Added Flametongue Seal (29270) as #1 -- +17 crit, +49 fire damage. BiS fire-specific offhand from Sha'tar Exalted.

## arcane-mage

### Reorders
- **back**: Shawl of Shifting Probabilities moved to #1 (was #2). Same reasoning as fire mage.
- **trinket1**: Icon of the Silver Crescent moved to #1 (was #2).
- **wand**: The Black Stalk moved to #1 (was #2).

### Additions
- **offhand**: Added Talisman of Kalecgos (29271) as #1 -- +14 int, +50 arcane damage. BiS arcane-specific offhand from The Consortium Exalted.

## frost-mage

### Reorders
- **ring1**: Seer's Signet moved to #1 (was #2). Higher raw SP.
- **wand**: The Black Stalk moved to #1 (was #2).

### Additions
- **neck**: Added Manasurge Pendant (29368) as #1 -- +22 int, +28 SP, +24 stam. Only had 2 neck options.
- **back**: Added Cloak of the Necropolis (23050) as #2 -- +11 int, +26 SP. Classic Naxx alternative.
- **ring1**: Added Ring of Cryptic Dreams (29367) as #2 -- +17 int, +23 SP, +16 stam.
- **ring2**: Replaced duplicate Seer's Signet with Cobalt Band of Tyrigosa (29352) as #1 and Ashyen's Gift as #2.
- **trinket2**: Added Shiffar's Nexus-Horn (28418) as #2 -- +30 crit rating.
- **offhand**: Added Sapphiron's Wing Bone (29269) as #1 -- +12 hit, +51 frost damage. BiS frost-specific offhand.

## shadow-priest

### Reorders
- **mainhand**: Gavel of Unearthed Secrets moved to #1 (was #2). Guides recommend it as top pick for shadow priests due to lower hit cap making raw SP from Eternium Runed Blade less necessary.
- **wand**: The Black Stalk moved to #1 (was #2).

### Additions
- **ring2**: Added Cobalt Band of Tyrigosa (29352) as #1 -- +19 stam, +17 int, +35 SP. Was a single-item slot.
- **offhand**: Added Khadgar's Knapsack (29273) as #2 -- +49 SP. Was a single-item slot.

## holy-priest

### Reorders
- **wrists**: Bindings of the Timewalker moved to #1 (was #2). Higher raw +healing (64 vs 46).
- **offhand**: Tears of Heaven moved to #1 (was #2). Higher raw +healing (75 vs 64) plus mp5.

### Additions
- **ring2**: Added Ring of Convalescence (29169) as #2 -- +15 int, +57 heal, +4 mp5. Was a single-item slot.
- **trinket2**: Added Lower City Prayerbook (30841) as #2 -- +70 heal plus mana cost reduction use effect. Was a single-item slot.
- **wand**: Added Wand of the Seer (30859) as #2 -- +8 int, +5 spi, +18 SP. Was a single-item slot.
